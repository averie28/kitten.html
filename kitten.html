Kitten Fun Facts

  • Kittens are born blind and deaf
  • The first year of a kitten's life is equivalent to 15 human years
  • Kittens get 26 teeth within 3 weeks
  • Kittens can sleep up to 20 hours a day

Developement of Kittens

  1. When they're first born they can't see or hear
  2. After 10-12 days their eyes open and their ears begin to unfold
  3. Weeks 3-4 kittens start to walk and grow teeth
  4. After 5 weeks they go from milks to solid foods
  5. Weeks 5 and 6 their ears and eyes are fully matured, and they can run, pounce, and start using the litter box
  6. At 8 weeks kittens are ready to leave their mothers and go to new homes
